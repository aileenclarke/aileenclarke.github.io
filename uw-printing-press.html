Maps for the University of Nebraska Printing Press

Tools: ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator

These maps are a selection of those produced for the 2023 edition of The Culture of Western Europe and the upcoming edition of Europe in the Sixteenth Century by George L. Mosse. These maps required the recreation of over a dozen maps produced for the original 1960s editions of Mosses' books. The maps were difficult to read in their original form and needed to be decoded and produced for modern printing capabilities, though still in grayscale.
