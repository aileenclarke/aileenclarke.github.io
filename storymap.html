The Future of Chocolate City, StoryMap

Tools: ArcGIS StoryMaps, ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator

This ArcGIS StoryMap looks at recent demographic changes in Washington, DC. Using census data, my story looks at population change by race from 1950 to 2020. I explored several metrics related to gentrification such as median household income and rent burden. The story identifies several example neighborhoods in the city and the ways they’ve changed or remained the same since DC’s Black population peaked in the 1970s.

View in full: https://arcg.is/0WGW4H0
