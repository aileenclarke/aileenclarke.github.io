Hi!

I'm Aileen.

I'm a cartographer currently living in Madison, Wisconsin where I completed my Masters in Cartography and GIS at the University of Wisconsin, Madison in December 2022. I'm currently Junior Developer with the USGS Web Informatics and Mapping team and an editor for the North American Cartographic Information Society's Atlas of Design, Version 7, which will begin accepting submissions in fall 2023.

I'm passionate about using maps and interactives as a means of storytelling. In my free time I enjoy making maps of DC, collecting weird vintage cookbooks, taking walks to pet the neighborhood cats, and biking with friends.

Contact me at aileenclarke4@gmail.com or find me on Github and LinkedIn.
